## Japanese Community Insights

### "餅は餅屋" (Mochi wa Mochiya) Philosophy

Japanese proverb applied to multi-agent systems: "The rice cake seller knows best" - each agent specializes in their domain:

| Agent | Role (役割) |
|-------|-------------|
| Sisyphus | 全体の指揮・タスク管理 |
| Oracle | 設計・デバッグ・戦略 |
| Frontend UI/UX Engineer | UI/UXの実装 |
| Librarian | ドキュメント調査・OSS調査 |

### Cost Management Strategy

From Japanese articles:
- Reuse existing subscriptions (Claude Pro/Max, ChatGPT Plus)
- Configure free models for non-critical agents
- Use Opus 4.5 only for main orchestration
