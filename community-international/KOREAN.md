## Korean Community Best Practices

1. **모델 분할 전략** - Use cheaper models (Gemini) for planning, expensive models (Claude) for implementation
2. **하이브리드 구성** - Mix local models (Ollama) with cloud APIs
3. **팀 단위 AI 지원** - Use oh-my-opencode for team-level AI support
